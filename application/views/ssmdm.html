About Company

The company was founded in 1998. The company is located in the PADSARI, VDC-1, RUPANDEHI, Nepal. The company employs approximately 65 labors and 15 office staff. We are manufacturer, exporter and importer of all kinds of food grains and pulses. Our main business is pulses. We have already sold our lentils in the third country like Sri Lanka, Dubai, Singapore, Bangladesh and other countries. We import Black Mapte, Desi Chick Peas, Popcorn, Green Peas, Yellow Peas, Red Lentils, Brown Eye Bean, Black Eye Bean, Kabuli Chick Peas etc. from Australia, Canada, Argentina, Brazil, Sri Lanka and other countries.
